Privacy Policy for HeyBaby

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the HeyBaby application ("Application") for mobile devices, created by Nadir Toprak ("Service Provider"). The Application is provided as a Freemium service and is intended for use "AS IS".

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This includes:

  • Device Information: IP address, operating system, and app usage statistics (time spent, pages visited).
  • User-Provided Content: To provide its core AI feature, the Service Provider requires access to your Email and User-Selected Media (Photos).

2. AI Data Processing and Third-Party Sharing

To provide our AI baby-image generation feature, the Application transmits user-provided photos to third-party AI infrastructure providers. This transmission only occurs after you grant explicit in-app consent.

A. What Data is Sent

  • The parent photos you select within the app

B. Who the Data is Sent To

  • fal.ai, Inc. — AI processing infrastructure
  • Google LLC — Gemini image generation model

C. Purpose and Protection

Data is transmitted solely to generate the requested AI image. These providers are contractually or legally prohibited from using your personal media for their own independent purposes. We do not use your personal data to train third-party AI models without your explicit consent.

3. Face Data Policy

The Application processes the parent photos you provide to generate an AI image of a predicted child. Faces in these photos are processed solely for that purpose.

  • No Biometric Identification: We do not use face data to identify, authenticate, or create biometric profiles of users.
  • Processing & Retention: Face data is processed on-the-fly by AI providers. We do not store face data beyond the time required to generate output. Partners must delete such data after processing (typically within 12 hours).

4. Third-Party Access for Analytics and Operations

The Application uses third-party services for analytics, monetization, and stability:

  • Infrastructure: Google Play Services, Firebase (Crashlytics & Analytics)
  • Monetization: AdMob, RevenueCat

5. User Consent and Opt-Out Rights

Before any photo is sent to an AI provider, the Application presents an in-app disclosure identifying what data is sent and to whom, and requires your explicit agreement to proceed.

  • Opt-Out: You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application.
  • Data & Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using the "Delete Account" function within the Application, or by contacting us at nadoappsupport@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.
